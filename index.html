  <p>Chorebid (“we”, “our”, “us”) provides a family chores app. This policy explains what we collect, why, and your choices.</p>


  <h2>Data We Collect</h2>

  <ul>

    <li><strong>Camera (QR scanning only):</strong> We request Camera permission solely to scan a parent’s QR code and link a child account to a family. We do not record or store photos or video.</li>

    <li><strong>Account info:</strong> Name, email, and password (via Firebase Authentication).</li>

    <li><strong>App data:</strong> Chores and family membership stored in Firebase Firestore.</li>

    <li><strong>Device/diagnostics:</strong> Basic logs or crash data (e.g., Firebase Crashlytics) to improve app stability.</li>

    <li><strong>Push notifications (optional):</strong> A device token to send updates about chores.</li>

  </ul>


  <h2>How We Use Data</h2>

  <ul>

    <li>Create and manage accounts and family groups.</li>

    <li>Allow parents to invite children and link them via QR code.</li>

    <li>Deliver app features (chores, rewards, notifications).</li>

    <li>Maintain security and improve reliability.</li>

  </ul>


  <h2>Sharing</h2>

  <p>We do not sell your data. We use service providers to operate the app (e.g., Google/Firebase for auth, database, functions, crash reporting) in accordance with their terms and the law.</p>


  <h2>Children’s Privacy</h2>

  <p>Chorebid is designed for family use. Child accounts are created/linked by a parent or guardian. If we learn a child account was created without parental involvement, we will remove it.</p>


  <h2>Retention & Deletion</h2>

  <p>We keep data while your account is active. You may request deletion at any time by contacting us (see below). Some data may persist in backups for a limited period.</p>


  <h2>Your Choices</h2>

  <ul>
