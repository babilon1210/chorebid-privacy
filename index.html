Chorebid – Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-08-23

Chorebid (“the App”) helps families assign, track, and reward chores. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

By using Chorebid, you agree to this Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

Who we are

Controller: Eitan Shahar
Contact: eitan1.shahar@gmail.com

What we collect

  • Account information: parent and child display names, parent email address, family ID.
  • Chore and activity data: chores you create, rewards, deadlines, status/progress (claimed/complete/verified/paid), and timestamps.
  • Identifiers for notifications: a Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) token to deliver push notifications.
  • Device & app info (limited): basic technical details (e.g., app version) required to run core features and messaging.
  • Camera (QR scanning only): when you join a family via QR code, the camera is used solely to read the code. No photos or videos are captured, saved, or uploaded.

How we use information

  • To create and manage accounts and families.
  • To assign, track, and display chore status within a family.
  • To link a user to a family via QR code scanning.
  • To send optional push notifications (e.g., updates on chores).
  • To maintain the security and reliability of the App.

Legal bases (EEA/UK users)

  • Contract: providing the App’s core features.
  • Legitimate interests: security, preventing misuse, and sending essential service messages.
  • Consent: optional push notifications (you can disable in device settings).

Sharing

  • Within your family: chore data and names are visible to members of the linked family group.
  • Service providers: we use Google Firebase (Authentication, Firestore, Cloud Functions, Cloud Messaging) to operate the App and deliver notifications.
  • We do not sell personal information.

Third-party services

  • Google / Firebase: Firebase Privacy & Security, Google Privacy Policy

Data retention

We retain account and family data for as long as your account is active. If you request deletion, we will delete or anonymize personal data unless we need to keep it to comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, or enforce agreements.

Children’s privacy

Chorebid is designed for family use. A parent or legal guardian must create the family and can create/link child profiles. We do not knowingly allow a child to create an independent account. Parents can review or delete child information by contacting us at eitan1.shahar@gmail.com.

Security

We use industry-standard safeguards and Firebase security rules to protect data. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we work to protect your information.

Your choices & rights

  • Access, update, delete: contact us to exercise your rights or use in-app options where available.
  • Notifications: you can disable push notifications in your device settings.
  • EEA/UK: you may have rights to access, rectify, erase, restrict, or object to processing and data portability. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local supervisory authority.

International transfers

We may process and store data on servers operated by our service providers (e.g., Google Firebase), which can be located outside your country. We rely on appropriate safeguards where required by law.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will change the “Last updated” date above and, where required, notify you in the app.
